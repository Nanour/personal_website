Digital Sales Corporation System (php, MySQL, js, css)

Digital Sales Online System is a relational database system that can run day-to-day transactions of the company. It is also referred to an on-line transaction processing system. The Digital Sales Corporation has 100 stores in different regions with average of 1000 customers per store. Digital Sales Online System (DSOS) contains all the information during day-to-day transactions such as store information, customer information, salesperson information and details of transaction. DSOS has three types of users, ‘manager’, ‘salesperson’ and ‘customer’. One cannot access the system without login. ‘Manager’ can access all the functions of the system such as browsing all the information, adding new salesperson, customer, products, and transactions, and updating and deleting current salesperson, customer and transactions. ‘Salesperson’ can access majority functions of the system such as browsing all the information, adding new products, customers, and transactions. ‘Customer’ can be added by ‘manager’ and ‘salesperson’, and customers can also register for the system by themselves. ‘Customer’ can access some functions of the system such as browsing products and stores information and adding new transactions.
