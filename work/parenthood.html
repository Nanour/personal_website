Planned Parenthood of Western PA(Android, MySQL, servlet, JSP),CodeFest held by Google

Steel City CodeFest 2014 is held by Google, Pittsburgh. Our team members are Jun Yin, Rui Cai, Chaoran Zhou, Ruonan Zhang.

Background


Planned Parenthood of Western PA's (PPWP) Peer Helpers program is a comprehensive sexuality education and pregnancy prevention program that trains students in grades 6-12. PPWP are launching an SMS hotline where teens can submit anonymous sexual health questions. Different from textlines where staff educators answer questions, they want high school-aged Peer Helpers to craft the responses. PPWP needs an app that would allow staff to oversee the process, filtering and approving all incoming/outgoing SMS, and students to independently craft answers from tablets and personal cell phones. The app should also include searching recommended responses to FAQ and an archive of SMS threads, researching questions through PPWP's website and etc.

Design


According to the requriments, we built a prototype model of the application. The activity diagram was shown below.



We developed a user-friendly interface for the Android Application and implemented core functions like information exchange between different types of users(using servlet) and information retrieval. We also created a website page and implemented the information exchange and monitor from the web-end.
