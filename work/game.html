3D Ball game


Based on code.

E: Stop
W: Forward
S: Backword
A: Left
D: Right
<- : Turn Left
-> : Turn Right
There are two kinds of balls, blue and pink. The character(which is also a ball) you can control is a smaller pink ball. Use 'w','s','a','d' to control the character, and use GLUT_KEY_RIGHT and GLUT_KEY_RIGHT to steer. The pink balls are targets, when you hit the ball, it will disappear, and you will get one score. The blue balls are 'walls' that you cannot pass across. When you hit blue balls, you will see you go back. The game is over when you find all target pink balls, and it will exit. I have used bounding volume to detect the collision(blue balls), and for the steer function(use GLUT_KEY_RIGHT and GLUT_KET_LEFT), I translate the ordinate.


The game was shown in perspective projection. On top left, an orthographic projection map was created.
