Diamond in the Rough

About the game


Diamonds in the Rough is a Kinect game developed in two weeks. It is a story of a carbon rock who learns about the meaning of friendships and helping and ultimately turns into a diamond. There are three levels in the game. The player will become the Mountain and rotate the ground with their hands (using Kinect detection) to help the carbon rock travel underground, overcome difficulties, meet other rocks, and finally erupt from a volcano becoming a diamond in the end.
