My Work - Game



Games with Cozmo
The goal of this project is to create new games for Anki's Cozmo robot so that Cozmo will arrive into consumers' lives with the ability to play a variety of games. The primary focus is to take advantage of Cozmo's capabilities in unique and engaging ways.

Jurassic Power Innovation Center (Project Joule)
Project joule is the fourth and final project created for the Energy Lab, an interactive learning space for middle-school students based at Elizabeth Forward School District and sponsored by Chevron. As the final installment, our goal is to synthesize solar, wind, and fossil fuel energies explored in the other experiences to encourage energy conservation habits and get students excited about science and technology.

BVW(Building Virtual World) Photobooth (Kinect 2, Unity3D, PHP)
BVW Photobooth is an interactive Kinect 2 experience developed in three weeks. Our team selected seven scenes from courses projects, and added interactive elements to these scenes. Players can choose either scene, and experience different interactivties. Four photos will be taken, and we will genereate animated photos(.gif) for the players in real-time. We also have a web site to show all photos. Players will get a code at the end of this experience. With the code, they can go to the web site to download, share on Facebook or email their photos.

Diamonds in the Rough (Kinect, Unity3D)
Diamonds in the Rough is a Kinect game developed in two weeks. It is a story of a carbon rock who learns about the meaning of friendships and helping and ultimately turns into a diamond. There are three levels in the game. The player will become the Mountain and rotate the ground with their hands (using Kinect detection) to help the carbon rock travel underground, overcome difficulties, meet other rocks, and finally erupt from a volcano becoming a diamond in the end.

Uni-Butt (Makey Makey, Unity3D)
A unicorn powered by rainbow farts rockets into space!!! Guests control unicorns to collect donuts (which are their energy), then unicorns fart rainbow to soar (run on clouds) and rocket into space, then come back to earth. Guests put on a huge fake unicorn butt and spank themselves to move. This 'WOW' experience is tailored for fun, and players will definately want to take selfies with our unicorns.

Float Me Up! (Oculus Rift, PlayStation Move, Unity3D)
A VR game developed with Oculus Rift and PlayStation Move. A well-escaping game using hammer by breaking right bricks.

Rain Dancer (Kinect, Unity3D)
Raindance to save the Mayan village from volcano! Mayan themeed one player Kinect dancing game with five simple gestures. Mayan art and sound will make the festival more diverse. Good game design will make all guests sweat and feel the ecstacy of dancing. Enjoy the atmosphere of Maya!!

Play Gomoku with Sound (Speech API, VB, photoshop)
Developed a Gomoku game with the Microsoft speech API and Visual Basic. Players can easily say ‘play’ to start the game, and speak two numbers(row and column) to locate the chessman. Since it is a traditional game, the interface has been designed in a very user-friendly way. Two characters on the left can easily show the players whose turn right now, and identify the color of the chessman. After one play win the game, a message with the results will pop-up on the screen, and say ‘play’ again to start another game.

Play Tetris with Your Body (Kinect API, C#, photoshop)
Developed a windows Kinect game Tetris which users can play it with their body. For example, both hands up to rotate the object, left hand up to move the object to left, two hands down to accelerate the speed of going down.

Memory Game(html,css,javascript)
A single memory game developed by javascript. The game will first ask users to input their name, after entering the name, the memory game starts. Click the square to flip to see whether two pictures match. Game over when all pictures are matched. Users playing history will be stored in cookie for users to review.

OpenGL single game(C, OpenGL)
Developed a 2D maze game, which users can drag the arrow in the maze to reach the destination. I have also created 3D environment for a 3D ball game and implemented bounding volume to detect collisions.
